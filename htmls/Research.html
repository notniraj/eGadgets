References

Miansai - Reference 1

The first design reference I took was from Miansai. The navigation bar and the cover section caught my attention. From it, I got the idea of implementing the nav bar and a cover section of my own where i could showcase my own deals.

Fitbit - Reference 2

The product tiles from the Fitbit products site was another website that caught my attention as i was exploring sites. The tiles were of a simple design and yet conveyed much information. From it, the product tiles used in my website were based off of.

Shopify - Reference 3

The Shopify website has a customer feedback page which shows multiple customer feedbacks from their customer. This customer feedback page gave me the idea for a customer reviews section in my website.

Daraz - Reference 4

Daraz's webpage has a lot of elements and being a good source of reference, I implemented the discover more page in my website with the help of Daraz's category section with modifications to fit the site's needs.

Paul Valentine - Reference 5

Paul Valentine's website has a pretty slick design. One of the things that was a good reference to take from the site was their footer section. Simple design with enough information linked. The footer section in my website was based Off of Paul Valentine's footer design.
